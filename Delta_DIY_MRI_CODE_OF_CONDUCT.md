# Delta DIY MRI -- Community Code of Conduct

The Delta DIY MRI community is committed to fostering an open,
collaborative, respectful, and inclusive environment for engineers,
scientists, students, clinicians, and contributors worldwide.

## Our Standards

We expect all contributors and participants to:

-   Communicate respectfully and professionally.
-   Welcome diverse perspectives and constructive technical debate.
-   Provide helpful feedback grounded in scientific rigor.
-   Credit contributions appropriately.
-   Follow laboratory and electrical safety standards when replicating
    hardware builds.

## Unacceptable Behavior

The following behaviors are not tolerated:

-   Harassment, discrimination, or exclusionary conduct.
-   Personal attacks, insults, or inflammatory comments.
-   Sexual harassment or unwelcome advances.
-   Intimidation, stalking, or sustained disruption.
-   Posting offensive or inappropriate content in issues, pull requests,
    discussions, or external channels.

## Scope

This Code applies to:

-   GitHub repositories, issues, pull requests, and discussions.
-   Workshop-related communications.
-   Social media channels connected to the project.
-   In-person workshops and affiliated events.

## Reporting

Concerns may be reported confidentially to the project maintainers or
workshop organizers. For NIH-supported events, participants may also
contact institutional compliance offices, NIH, or the HHS Office for
Civil Rights.

We aim to build not only MRI systems---but also a collaborative
scientific community grounded in respect, safety, and openness.
