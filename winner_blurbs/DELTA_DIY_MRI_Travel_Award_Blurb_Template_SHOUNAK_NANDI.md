## SHOUNAK NANDI, M.Sc.

**Affiliation:** ALBERT EINSTEIN COLLEGE OF MEDICINE 
**Current role:** Study Coordinator in the Department of Radiology 
**Location:** Bronx, New York City, USA
**Workshop interests:** Data acquisition | Reconstruction 

I am a Study Coordinator working in the field of Neuroimaging and MRI. My current projects include group-level image analysis of pediatric cancer survivors, detection of microstructural changes in white matter, analysis of diffusion and functional MRI, and detection of low-field MRI textural biomarkers. At the DELTA DIY MRI Workshop, I aim to gain hands-on experience with low-field MRI acquisition and reconstruction algorithms and to translate this knowledge into the image acquisition and processing pipeline across different neuropathological cohorts. I am also excited to work on improving image quality in low-field MRI using different post-processing techniques like super-resolution.
